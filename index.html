Clinical Intelligence

Derma-Vision utilizes a sophisticated Dual-Stream EfficientNet-B7 architecture. Unlike standard classifiers, our network simultaneously processes raw RGB pixels alongside offline-computed CTE Maps (Color, Texture, and Edge saturation), fusing natural visual patterns with strict mathematical gradients.

Spatial Feature Localization

Malignancy probabilities shift significantly based on the anatomical origin of the lesion. Derma-Vision utilizes a custom Tabular Multi-Layer Perceptron (MLP) to weigh the visual inference against geographical prior probabilities.

  • Head & Neck: High sun exposure, distinct texture.
  • Torso: Largest surface area, common for nodular variants.
  • Extremities: Arms (Upper) and Legs (Lower).
  • Acral Regions: Palms & Soles (distinct ridge patterns).
