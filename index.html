Anatomical Intelligence

Malignancy probabilities shift based on anatomical origin. Derma-Vision's Tabular MLP weighs visual inference against geographical prior probabilities encoded during training.

  • Head & Neck: High UV exposure, distinct keratinocyte texture signatures.
  • Torso: Largest surface area, nodular and superficial spreading variants.
  • Extremities: Upper (Arms) and Lower (Legs) with distinct vascular patterns.
  • Acral Regions: Palms & Soles — unique ridge patterns, higher acral lentiginous risk.
