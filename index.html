Clinical Intelligence

Spatial Feature Localization

Malignancy probabilities shift based on the anatomical origin. Derma-Vision utilizes a Tabular MLP to weigh visual inference against geographical prior probabilities.

  • Head & Neck: High sun exposure, distinct texture.
  • Torso: Largest surface area, nodular variants.
  • Extremities: Arms (Upper) and Legs (Lower).
  • Acral Regions: Palms & Soles (ridge patterns).
