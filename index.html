The Science of Detection

Melanoma is the most serious type of skin cancer, developing in the cells (melanocytes) that produce melanin. Early detection is paramount; when identified in its localized stages, the 5-year survival rate exceeds 99%. Derma-Vision serves as an advanced computational second-opinion for dermatological analysis.

Core Architecture

Derma-Vision utilizes a sophisticated Dual-Stream EfficientNet-B7 ensemble. It does not merely look at a standard photograph. The network simultaneously processes raw RGB pixels alongside offline-computed CTE Maps (Color, Texture, and Edge saturation), fusing visual patterns with strict mathematical boundaries.
